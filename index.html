Shuttle Schedule

The shuttle goes up and down the mountain hourly, sunrise to sunset, with a mid-day break, every day, all season long.

Shuttle leaves for Cadillac’s summit, from Bar Harbor, at the top of each hour and down from the Summit on the half hour. Boarding is 5 - 10 minutes prior to leaving, 1st come, 1st serve.

*All schedules are subject to change and are approximations. They can vary 5-10 minutes, depending on traffic and weather. Please check website for updates, especially in extreme weather.

Ticket Pricing

One-way or round-trip tickets available:

  • One-way ticket: $10.00
  • Round-trip ticket: $20.00
  • Dogs: $5.00

Cash is preferred, but we also accept cards. Tips are appreciated.

MDI locals and MDI workers receive 1/2 price. Must have ID and/or paystub with ID.

Pickup and Drop-off Location

Pickup and drop-off in Bar Harbor is located at:

317 Main St., 2 blocks up from ball park at the Sunoco Station, in between Pat's Pizza and Everyday Joe's.
